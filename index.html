Wonderbloom Redesign

The Wonderbloom preschool and non-profit website was an eyesore for users & parents. The new redesigned website will make the search for a new preschool smooth and easy, leading to a confident, informed decision.

Timeframe: 3 Week - Role: UX/UI Designer

Software Used: Figma, FigJam, Google suite, Trello

Wonderbloom was the first nature-based preschool to be established in Salt Lake City, UT. They were inspired by the philosophy of European "Forest Schools," where children learn away from screens and in a more natural environment.
I identified that our users would be mostly parents - whether they are looking for a preschool or already have their child enrolled with Wonderbloom. So the 3 user interviews done were with parents. These parents and users needed a better way to find the information that is most important, such as tuition prices, schedules and signing up for newsletters.

User Persona

Using our user interview information, the affinity diagram & empathy map created, we have our user persona, Ashley!

For parents looking for the perfect preschool, Wonderbloom’s redesigned website will make the search smooth and easy, leading to a confident, informed decision.

How might we help parents researching new preschools in the Salt Lake area easily and efficiently find the calendar, pricing, newsletter enrollment, & curriculum information on the Wonderbloom website?

By making the preschool’s website layout and navigation intuitive, we hypothesize it will be easier for parents and all users to find the information they need when selecting a preschool.

After a heuristic evaluation, it was determined that the Wonderbloom website could be improved with better color usability for accessibility and just overall visual appeal as well as a better navigation and a more standardized font and alignment across the pages and information. The card sorting method was used to organize the navigation into a more intuitive order with four new sections leading to a new sitemap & user flow.

New Site Map

The redesign now includes natural colors, patterns, and an organic feel to emphasize Wonderbloom’s mission to create a holistic approach to early childhood education.

This non profit website has so much potential still even after the redesign. If the project were to continue, next steps could be to implement a new app for users. Wonderbloom is pushing the envelope of what we know preschools to be, so it would be incredible bringing them into the spotlight as they deserve.
